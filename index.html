Game rules

  • Players should begin by filling in their names.
  • Any player can choose to start the game.
  • In one round, players can roll the die as many times as they wish.
  • If a player rolls a one, their cumulative score for that round is automatically reset to zero. Game moves to the opponent.
  • A player can choose to be safe with their points, hence pause the game and pass to their opponent. Here when the game is passed back to them and they get a 1-roll in that game, they still get to keep their initial score.
  • Finally, a winner should be declared once one of the player hits 100 points of overall score!
